## Introduction

Welcome to AI Wallpaper ("we", "our", "us"), an AI-powered wallpaper generation service available at [aiwallpaper.best](https://aiwallpaper.best). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our service.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Account Information
- **What we collect**: Email address, name, and authentication credentials when you create an account.
- **Purpose**: To manage your account, authenticate your identity, and provide customer support.

### 2. AI Generation Data
- **What we collect**: Text prompts you submit, reference images you upload, and AI-generated wallpaper outputs.
- **Purpose**: To provide the wallpaper generation service and display your generation history.

### 3. Credits and Payment Data
- **What we collect**: Credit balance, credit purchase records, subscription status, and payment transaction references.
- **Purpose**: To manage your credits, process payments, and handle billing inquiries. We do not store your full credit card number — payment processing is handled by our third-party payment provider (Creem).

### 4. Uploaded Content
- **What we collect**: Images you upload for image-to-image generation or as reference material.
- **Purpose**: To process your wallpaper generation requests. Uploaded images are stored in cloud object storage and are associated with your account.

### 5. Usage and Device Information
- **What we collect**: Pages visited, features used, device type, browser type, and general usage patterns.
- **Purpose**: To analyze and improve our service, optimize performance, and provide analytics.

### 6. Cookies and Tracking
- **What we collect**: Essential session cookies and analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity).
- **Purpose**: To maintain your session, remember preferences, and understand how visitors use our site.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the AI wallpaper generation service
- Process credit purchases and manage subscriptions
- Send service-related notifications (e.g., low credit alerts)
- Respond to your support requests
- Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit.
- Uploaded images and generated wallpapers are stored in cloud object storage (Cloudflare R2) with access controls.
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.
- However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate our platform:

| Service | Purpose | Data Accessed |
|---------|---------|--------------|
| Replicate | AI image generation | Text prompts, reference images |
| Creem | Payment processing | Email, payment details |

- We do not claim ownership of the AI-generated outputs you create.
- You are responsible for ensuring that your text prompts and uploaded content do not violate any laws or third-party rights.
- The AI models used are developed by their respective providers. Use of these models is subject to their terms of service.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:
- With service providers who assist in operating our platform (as listed above)
- To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests
- To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users

## Data Retention

- Account data is retained while your account is active and for a reasonable period after deletion.
- AI generation history and uploaded content are retained until you delete them or close your account.
- Payment records are retained as required by law.
- Analytics data is anonymized and retained for up to 26 months.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal information we hold about you
- Request correction of inaccurate information
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Opt out of analytics tracking through your browser settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [support@aiwallpaper.best](mailto:support@aiwallpaper.best).
