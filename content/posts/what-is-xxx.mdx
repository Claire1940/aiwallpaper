## How Does It Work?

1. **Describe Your Vision**: Enter a text prompt describing the wallpaper you want. For example: "A serene mountain landscape at sunset with a lake reflection"

3. **Generate**: Click the generate button and wait for your unique wallpaper

4. **Download**: Save your AI-generated wallpaper in high resolution
