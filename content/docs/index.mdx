## What is AI Wallpaper?

AI Wallpaper is an AI-powered wallpaper generation service that lets you create stunning, unique wallpapers with just a text prompt. Simply describe the wallpaper you want, choose an AI model, and generate high-resolution wallpapers for your desktop, phone, or any device.

## Features

- **Text to Image**: Describe your wallpaper idea in words and let AI generate it
- **Image to Image**: Upload a reference image and transform it with AI
- **Multiple AI Models**: Choose from advanced AI models optimized for wallpaper generation
- **High Resolution**: Generate wallpapers in high resolution suitable for any screen
- **Credits System**: Flexible credit-based pricing with one-time packs and subscriptions
- **Commercial Use**: Generated wallpapers can be used for personal and commercial projects

## Getting Started

1. Create an account at [aiwallpaper.best](https://aiwallpaper.best)
